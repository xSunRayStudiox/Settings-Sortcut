Accessing Search Settings

  1. Open Settings: Start by opening the Settings app on your Android device.
  2. Navigate to Search: Scroll down and select "Search" or "Google" depending on your device.
  3. Search Settings: Tap on "Search" or "Search settings" to access the search settings menu.

Managing Search Settings

  1. SafeSearch: Enable or disable SafeSearch to filter explicit results in your searches.
  2. Languages: Select the languages you want to include in your search results.
  3. Region: Choose the region for your search results to get more relevant information.
  4. Voice Search: Configure voice search settings, including language, speech output, and personalized results.
  5. Accounts & Privacy: Manage settings related to your Google account and search history.
  6. Notifications: Customize the notifications you receive from the search app.

Using Search Settings

  1. Customizing Search Results: Adjust the settings to customize the search results you see based on your preferences.
  2. Managing Search History: You can view and delete your search history under the "Accounts & Privacy" section.
  3. Personalized Results: Enable or disable personalized search results based on your activity and interests.
Note: Adjusting your search settings can help improve the relevance and safety of your search results. Take time to explore and configure these settings to suit your preferences.
